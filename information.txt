Veronika Resort – Overview

Veronika Resort is a luxury property located in the scenic Shivalik Ranges near the famous Jatoli Shiv Temple (Asia’s highest Shiv temple). Just 6 km from Solan city, the resort offers a peaceful retreat with modern comforts and panoramic valley views.

Location & Accessibility
- Address: Jatoli, Rajgarh Road, Solan-173212, Himachal Pradesh
- Coordinates: 30°52′45″N, 77°07′41″E

- 3 km from nearby schools, hospitals, markets, and offices
- Located on a State Highway (PWD road) connecting Solan with Rajgarh
